FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is this for box lacrosse or field lacrosse?

We offer programs and camps for both box lacrosse and field lacrosse.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We do our best to make our programs affordable for everybody through payment plans.

Are there any additional costs that we should be made aware of?

Travel insurance is the only fee you will be required to pay on top of our sticker price. There are options to pay for additional equipment, apparel, etc. But those are not required for anybody.

What age groups do you offer programs for?

Our marquee programs are for U-13, U-15 and U-18. We do offer camps and clinics for younger age groups as well.

Do you have a girls program?

We do not offer any programs designed for girls lacrosse; however, girls are welcome to join our camps or tryout for our teams.

Where are the practices?

Practice locations may vary, but we are aiming to host all sessions in Langley and Maple Ridge.

Where are the tournaments?

It varies based on the age group; please refer to the "Field Programs" page for more details.

Do you provide travel?

Grade 11 and 12 players have the option to purchase the travel package; a chaperone must accompany the younger players to the travel tournaments.

How are the teams picked?

Our world-class coaching staff will select the team based on each evaluation during the tryout process.

Will this conflict with our association's schedule?

We are not a sanctioned program which means that our scheduled practices, camps and events may conflict with your club team's schedule.
